## CONTENT STRUCTURE (Optimized for Max's Top Performers)

Based on analytics: Articles with 138.0 and 126.0 engagement scores share these patterns.

---

### THE WINNING STRUCTURE

**1. Hook (0-50 words)**

- Status threat + paradox

- No warm-up, no context-setting

- Specific numbers or historical anchor

**2. Scene Drop (50-200 words)**

- Historical or concrete example

- Place + time + sensory detail

- "France, 1737. Antonio Stradivarius..."

**3. The Problem Made Visible (200-400 words)**

- Universal principle extracted from scene

- Short sentences for impact

- "There it was."

**4. Research Anchor (400-600 words)**

- Named researcher + specific finding

- Weave naturally, not academic style

- "Cognitive neuroscientists Nico Bunzeck and Emrah DÃ¼zel proved this with MRI imaging."

**5. The Mechanism (600-1000 words)**

- How this works specifically

- Lists (target: 4-6 in this section)

- Decision logic, frameworks, taxonomies

**6. Application/Case Study (1000-1500 words)**

- Before/After contrast

- Specific numbers

- Named frameworks

- Lists (target: 4-8 in this section)

**7. The Extraction/Takeaway (1500-2000 words)**

- What reader can do

- Tools, prompts, frameworks

- Lists (target: 4-6 in this section)

**8. Close (final 100-200 words)**

- No summary

- Final concrete insight or call to action

- End on mechanism, not motivation

---

### STRUCTURAL METRICS

| Element | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Total word count | 2,400-3,400 |
| Total lists | 8-18 |
| Avg paragraph words | 6-10 |
| Headings | Optional (top performers use 0) |
| Short paragraphs (<20 words) | 40%+ of paragraphs |

---

### LIST REQUIREMENTS

- Each list item: scannable in <5 seconds

- Lists must be substantive (not padding)

- Use lists for: frameworks, steps, contrasts, taxonomies

- Don't use lists for: narrative, emotional beats, story

---

### PARAGRAPH RHYTHM

Alternate between:

- 1-sentence paragraphs (punch)

- 2-3 sentence paragraphs (develop)

- Occasional 4+ sentence paragraph (depth)

Never:

- Multiple long paragraphs in sequence

- Wall of text without break

- Paragraph averaging >12 words
